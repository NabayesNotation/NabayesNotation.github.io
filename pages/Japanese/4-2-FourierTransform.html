確率解析

  • \(E,F\) を可微分多様体とする．２変数関数 \(f:E\times F\to\mathbb{R}\) について，

\[\begin{align*} C^{1,2}(E\times F)&:=\bigg\{f:E\times F\to\mathbb{R}\;\bigg|\:\substack{\forall_{y\in F}\;f(-,y)\in C^1(E)\\\forall_{x\in E}\;f(x,-)\in C^2(F)}\bigg\} \end{align*}\]

と表す．5

作用素

  • The domain of the operator \(T\) is denoted by \(\mathcal{D}(T)\).

  • The graph of the operator \(T:X\to Y\) is denoted by \[ \mathcal{G}(T):=\left\{x\oplus Tx\in X\oplus Y\mid x\in\mathcal{D}(T)\right\}. \]
